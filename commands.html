💡 Tips & Tricks

  • CX-3 input fields — Enter any 2 values, leave the rest blank; the missing value is computed.
  • Fuel type — Tap 100LL or Jet A to switch density; tap Custom to enter an exact value.
  • W&B station rows — Click +STATION to add more rows, ✕ to remove.
  • Graph auto-scale — Leave Y-range blank for auto Y-axis; the plot fills the viewable area.
  • 3D graphing — Use x and y variables in the expression. Drag to rotate the 3D surface.
  • PDF annotations — Survival across saves: annotations are embedded into the downloaded PDF file.
  • PDF zoom — Ctrl+Scroll on a trackpad gives smooth Acrobat-style zoom.
  • CAD files — STEP files use an in-browser parser; complex assemblies may take a few seconds.
  • Mobile — Pages are responsive. Use numeric keypad on touch devices for quick input.
  • Dark mode — All tools feature a dark, low-glare theme optimized for flight deck and late-night use.
