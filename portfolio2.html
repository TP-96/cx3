How I Work

Three principles. No shortcuts.

01

Understand the problem first

Every project starts with the user's need — not the technology. Whether it's a client with mobility restrictions or a team building a robotic arm, the requirements define the solution.

02

Design, prototype, iterate

CAD models go through revision. Parts get remade. Code gets rewritten. Each cycle moves the design closer to something that works in the real world.

03

Simple is better

The simplest solution is often the best one — a principle reinforced through every prototype iteration and project setback I've learned from.
